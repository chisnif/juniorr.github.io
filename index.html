Hi, my name is Chisom Echebelem Junior and I am also a digital artist and a 2D animator based in Lagos. I have always had a passion for art and technology, and I love using digital tools to create unique and visually stunning pieces.

As a digital artist and animator, I specialize in character design, concept art, illustration, 2D animation, motion graphics and More. I enjoy experimenting with different styles and techniques to bring my ideas to life, and I am always looking for new ways to push the boundaries of what is possible with digital art.
